Our Markets in Sierra Leone

Fortizo Technologies SL operates across key regions in Sierra Leone, providing digital solutions, branding, events, and market research.

Key Markets

We work across various regions and sectors in Sierra Leone to support businesses and communities with technology and services.

Freetown (HQ)

Our base in the capital, focusing on fintech, ecommerce, and digital platforms.

Northern Region

Supporting Bombali, Port Loko, and Tonkolili with agritech and market research.

Southern Region

Providing branding and event services in Bo, Bonthe, Moyamba, and Pujehun.

Eastern Region

Offering e-learning and digital solutions in Kenema, Kono, and Kailahun.

Tech Ecosystem

Collaborating with startups in fintech, agritech, and edtech to support Sierra Leone’s digital economy.

Organizing tech events and workshops to engage communities across Sierra Leone.
