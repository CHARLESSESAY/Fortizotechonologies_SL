FAQs

What services does Fortizo offer? +

We offer Meeting Facilitation, Market Research, Marketing & Branding, and Digital Solutions & E-Learning. Visit our Services page for more details.

We operate across Freetown (HQ), Western Area, Northern Region (Bombali, Port Loko, Tonkolili), Southern Region (Bo, Bonthe, Moyamba, Pujehun), and Eastern Region (Kenema, Kono, Kailahun). See our Market page for details.

Our portfolio includes e-learning platforms, branding campaigns, tech events, market research, and digital payment solutions. Check out our Portfolio page for examples.

Does Fortizo offer career opportunities? +

Yes, we’re always looking for talent to join our innovative projects. Visit our Careers page for current openings or email us at info@fortizo.sl.

What is the Sierra Leone tech ecosystem like? +

Sierra Leone’s tech ecosystem in 2025 is vibrant, with ~2.32 million internet users and a focus on digital transformation. Key sectors include Ecommerce, Fintech, Agri-tech, and AI. Notable initiatives include the Sierra Leone Innovates: Digital Government Summit (June 4-6, 2025) and SLinT Tech Expo 2025. For more, ask our chatbot or visit our Newsroom.

How can I get support from Fortizo? +

Check our Support page for resources or submit a request. You can also use our chatbot or email info@fortizo.sl for assistance.
